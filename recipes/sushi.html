Sushi

Back to home

Ingredients needed:

  • Sushi Rice
  • Salmon
  • Avocado
  • Seaweed Paper

Steps to make:

  1. Lay 15cmx15cm of Seaweed Paper on the table
  2. Spread out 1cm layer of cooked Sushi Rice all over the Seaweed Paper
  3. Layer a row of salmon across sheet
  4. Layer a row of sliced avocado across sheet
  5. roll sheet into a roll and slice Sushi roll evey 5cm
